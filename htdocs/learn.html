About the Fund

Mary Pott taught for more than three decades at Hamilton Elementary School, and she touched the lives of hundreds of people during her time there. Mary's family established the Fund in her memory, after her sudden death in February 2002, with the hundreds of donations given by her friends, family, colleagues, former students, and many others.

The Mary Pott Elementary Education Fund is directed by Mary's family, with assets managed by the Allegan County Community Foundation. The ACCF has been instrumental in the fund's success and growth. The goal of the Fund is to provide assistance to students in the elementary schools of the Hamilton, Michigan school district, particularly in the areas of reading and the arts.

While the Fund has a strong base, continuing and expanding its work requires financial support. Please consider making a donation. Thank you!
